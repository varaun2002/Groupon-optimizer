## Deal Value Assessment

The Groupon deal at $58.50 for single admission (22% off the $75 listed price) is competitive but context-dependent. The merchant's direct price is $60 per person, making this deal only $1.50 cheaper. However, H Mart sells admission tickets at $50, which undercuts this Groupon by $8.50. The deal claims 'up to 32% discount' in the title but the actual discount shown is 22% off $75 (savings of $16.50), or potentially 32% if calculating against the base $75 price point. Historical Reddit data indicates prices have increased significantly—admission was previously $35 with Groupons at $25, suggesting the market has shifted upward. For budget-conscious visitors, purchasing H Mart tickets at $50 remains the best alternative; for convenience, this Groupon is marginally better than direct merchant pricing ($60) but represents declining value compared to historical pricing.
