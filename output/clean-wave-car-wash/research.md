## Deal Value Assessment

The Groupon deal at $21.60 for one full service wash (43% discount from $38 merchant direct price) or $53.10 for three washes (53% discount from $114) represents solid value compared to disclosed competitor pricing. Royal Wash charges $17.99 per single wash, making Clean Wave's Groupon single-wash price of $21.60 approximately 20% higher per wash. However, Clean Wave's comprehensive interior and exterior service with ceramic sealant and graphene-infused coating appears more premium than basic single washes. The three-wash bundle at $53.10 ($17.70 per wash via Groupon) undercuts Royal Wash's $17.99 single-wash rate. One Yelp reviewer noted the full service at $14 with tip when using Groupon. El Car Wash and Soapy Shark offer unlimited monthly plans (pricing not fully disclosed) which could provide better long-term value for frequent washers. For occasional users seeking premium interior-exterior service, the Groupon deal provides meaningful savings versus the $38 direct price.
