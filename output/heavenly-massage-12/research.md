## Deal Value Assessment

The Groupon deal offers competitive pricing compared to Chicago market rates. At $89.99 for a 60-minute massage (33% off stated $135 price), Heavenly Massage's Groupon price is below Urbano Oasis Massage's $145 standard rate for the same duration and comparable to entry-level Chicago massage pricing of $70-$120 cited by Groupon's own market data. However, the stated original price of $135 is significantly higher than Heavenly Massage's own advertised direct pricing of $75 for a 60-minute session found on their website, suggesting the 'original price' is inflated. The 90-minute option at $125.99 (28% off) is reasonable compared to Urbano Oasis's $210 rate. Couples massages at $161.99 for 60 minutes represent better value given standard market rates. The CBD oil enhancement option at $98.99 for 60 minutes (40% off $165) adds functional differentiation. For customers willing to use the 'GLOWUP' coupon code, prices drop further to $81.49 (60-min) and $113.99 (90-min), improving value proposition.
