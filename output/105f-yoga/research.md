## Deal Value Assessment

The Groupon deal represents strong value relative to 105F Yoga's direct pricing and competitive market rates. For the single class option: Groupon price is $22.50 (36% off $35 retail), significantly cheaper than Coconut Yoga Chicago's $25 drop-in rate and competitive with Big Shoulders Yoga's $15 online classes, though 105F includes mat and towel rental bundled. For the 3-week unlimited option: Groupon price is $89.10 (29% off $125 retail), which breaks down to approximately $12.72 per week or $3.18 per class assuming 10 classes. This undercuts Yoga Now's $69/month first-time offer ($17.25/week) and is significantly less than Bloom Yoga Studio's $129/month studio membership ($32.25/week). The deal is particularly competitive for new students given the restrictions exclude clients active in the past 12 months, positioning this as a genuine new-customer acquisition offer rather than a retention discount.

## Category Benchmark

Typical discount range for **Yoga Classes**: Hot yoga classes in Chicago typically offer 50-70% off introductory packages on Groupon, with single drop-in classes ranging $15-$25 and unlimited monthly memberships $69-$149. 105F Yoga's 29-36% discount is slightly below the category average but competitive given the bundled mat/towel rental and multi-location validity.
